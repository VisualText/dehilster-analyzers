After the long flight finally landed, the tired travelers wandered through the crowded terminal toward the noisy baggage hall. Outside the wide glass doors, dozens of yellow taxis waited patiently along the wet and busy street. The narrow mountain road climbed steadily past green terraced fields and small villages with red clay roofs. When they reached the ancient temple at the summit, a cool wind carried the deep sound of distant bells. They rested for a while on the worn stone steps and quietly watched the golden light fade behind the hills.
