The committee carefully reviewed the lengthy proposal that the two largest departments had jointly submitted last spring. After several long meetings, the members agreed that the original budget could not cover the ambitious new program. They therefore recommended that the council reduce the planned spending and postpone the construction of the second building. The chairman, who had served the organization for many difficult years, reluctantly accepted the revised plan. Once the final vote was counted, the surprised reporters quickly carried the unexpected news to the waiting crowd.
