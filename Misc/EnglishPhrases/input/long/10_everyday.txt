Early on Saturday, my older brother and I drove the rusty truck to the busy farmers market downtown. We bought ripe tomatoes, fresh bread, and a small jar of dark honey from a friendly local vendor. On the way home, we stopped beside the river and ate our simple lunch under a tall shady tree. While the warm afternoon slowly passed, the children played near the water and the dogs chased the rolling ball. Before the sun finally set, we packed our things and drove the long quiet road back to the farm.
