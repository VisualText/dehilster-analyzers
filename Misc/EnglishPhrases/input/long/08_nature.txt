Deep within the silent forest, tall ancient trees stretched their twisted branches toward the narrow strips of pale sky. A clear mountain stream tumbled over smooth round stones and gathered in a quiet pool beneath the mossy bank. Small bright birds darted between the ferns while a curious deer watched the strangers from the shadows. When the afternoon rain began to fall, thousands of tiny drops drummed softly on the broad green leaves. By evening the cool air smelled of wet earth, and a thin mist crept slowly along the winding valley floor.
