The detective slowly entered the dark library, where a single broken lamp still flickered above the heavy oak desk. Scattered papers covered the dusty floor, and a faint smell of cold smoke lingered near the open window. She noticed that the locked drawer had been forced open with some sharp and narrow tool. Although nothing valuable seemed to be missing, a small golden key lay hidden beneath the torn red carpet. As the storm grew louder outside, she carefully placed the strange key into a clear plastic bag.
