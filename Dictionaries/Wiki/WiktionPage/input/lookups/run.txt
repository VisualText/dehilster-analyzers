===Etymology===
{{root|en|ine-pro|*h₃er-}}
From {{inh|en|enm|runnen}}, {{m|enm|rennen|t=to run}}, alteration (due to the past participle {{m|enm|runne}}, {{m|enm|runnen}},
{{m|enm|yronne}}) of {{cog|enm|rinnen|t=to run}}, from {{inh|en|ang|rinnan}}, {{m|ang|iernan|t=to run}} and {{der|en|non|rinna|t=to run}}, both from {{inh|en|gem-pro|*rinnaną|t=to run}} (compare also {{m|gem-pro|*rannijaną|t=to make run}}), from {{der|en|ine-pro|*h₃reyH-|t=to boil, churn}}.

Cognate with {{cog|sco|rin||to run}}, {{cog|fy|rinne||to walk, march}}, {{cog|nl|rennen||to run, race}}, {{cog|gsw|ränne|t=to run}}, {{cog|de|rennen||to run, race}}, {{m|de|rinnen||to flow}}, ''[[Rhein]]'', {{cog|da|rende||to run}}, {{cog|sv|ränna||to run}}, {{cog|sv|rinna||to flow}}, {{cog|is|renna||to flow}}. Non-Germanic cognates include {{cog|sq|rend||to run, run after}}. See {{m|en|random}}.
